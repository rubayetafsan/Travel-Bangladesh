Bangladesh Tour

Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Bangladesh, renowned for its beautiful beaches, scenic views, and rich cultural heritage. A Cox's Bazar tour typically includes visits to some of the top attractions in the area, such as:Cox's Bazar Beach: The beach is the main attraction in Cox's Bazar, with its long stretch of sandy coastline and crystal-clear waters. Visitors can enjoy activities such as swimming, sunbathing, and beachside strolls.

Sundarban

A Sundarban tour is a unique and unforgettable experience, as it takes visitors to the world's largest mangrove forest and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, located in the southwestern part of Bangladesh. The Sundarbans is home to the Bengal tiger, saltwater crocodile, and a vast array of bird species, making it a paradise for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.A typical Sundarban tour would include the following:Boat ride through the canals: The tour usually begins with a boat ride.

Mahasthangar

Mahasthangarh is an ancient archaeological site located in Bogra district, Bangladesh. It is considered to be one of the most important historical sites in the country and attracts a large number of tourists every year. A typical Mahasthangarh tour would include the following:Arrival: The tour would usually start with your arrival in Bogra district. You can travel there by car or bus from the capital city Dhaka, which is approximately 220 km away.

Sajek

Sajek is a beautiful hill district located in the north of Bangladesh, near the border with India. A typical Sajek tour would include the following:Arrival: The tour would usually start with your arrival in the nearby town of Khagrachari. You can travel there by car or bus from the capital city Dhaka, which is approximately 250 km away.

Sylhet

Sylhet is a city located in the northeastern part of Bangladesh, known for its scenic beauty, tea gardens, and cultural heritage. A typical Sylhet tour would include the following:Arrival: The tour would usually start with your arrival in Sylhet. You can travel there by car or bus from the capital city Dhaka, which is approximately 240 km away. There is also an airport in Sylhet, with regular flights from Dhaka.Visiting the tea gardens: Sylhet is known for its tea gardens.

National Memorial

The National Memorial is a historical site located in Savar, Bangladesh, dedicated to the martyrs of the Bangladesh Liberation War of 1971. A typical National Memorial tour would include the following:Arrival: The tour would usually start with your arrival in Dhaka, the capital city of Bangladesh. You can travel to the National Memorial in Savar by car or bus, which is approximately 35 km away.

National Zoo

The National Zoo in Bangladesh, also known as the Mirpur Zoo, is a popular tourist attraction located in the Mirpur area of Dhaka, the capital city of Bangladesh. A typical National Zoo tour would include the following:Arrival: The tour would usually start with your arrival in Dhaka. You can travel to the National Zoo by car or public transportation, which is located in the Mirpur area of the city.Visiting the National Zoo: The main attraction of the tour is the National Zoo itself. It is a large zoo with a variety of animals from Bangladesh and around the world.

Shat Gambuj Masjid

The Shat Gambuj Mosque, also known as the Sixty Dome Mosque, is a historical mosque located in Bagerhat, a city in southern Bangladesh. A typical Shat Gambuj Masjid tour would include the following: Arrival: The tour would usually start with your arrival in Bagerhat, which can be reached by car or public transportation from the nearby city of Khulna. Visiting the Shat Gambuj Mosque: The main attraction of the tour is the Shat Gambuj Mosque itself.
